Designer 1

I’m Ivonne Ronderos, and I started IN Interiors in 2004.
Growing up in a family of entrepreneurs inspired me to create a different kind of interior design firm, one that puts empathy and service at the core—and delivers good design as well. Almost 15 years later, I’m still a DKORista at heart! I get excited to begin each new project, love to see the look on our clients’ faces during the “big reveal” moment, and for everything in between.

What inspires me?
What I love the most about what I do is the way it changes people’s lives (our clients and our team members). My colleagues, clients and all of the amazing vendors and the extended team we get to work with always inspires me to be better.
